To deploy/open the app:
=========================
Simply open index.html on any browser. Use latest browser versions for best results. Tested on Microsoft Edge, Firefox, Opera, Chrome.

To operate the app:
====================
Enter respective words in the input boxes.
Press 'Submit' button to display your love story. All words must be entered before submitting.
Press 'Clear' button to clear all input boxes as well as the displayed story.
In order to update entered word in the story without clearing, simply change the word in the respective box and submit.
Finally, don't forget to spread love via Topcoder :)
