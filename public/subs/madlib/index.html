Topcoder MadLib

On the Valentine's Day, Topcoder announced new challenge series that offered 250 dollars in rewards.

The challenge was to develop algorithms that can be used to identify lonely developers.

Hundreds of lonely developers signed up to participate in the challenge.

It is very easy to identify lonely developers as their work is very late.

But the winner got cats as his reward instead.

In the end, Topcoder eliminated loneliness and made the world a better place.
