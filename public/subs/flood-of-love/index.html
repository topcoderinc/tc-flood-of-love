Wanna know what Mad Libs is all about?

We've all probably played Mad Libs. One asks friends around for different words which can be nouns, adjectives, parts of the body, animals, fruits, colors or anything you like really! And then plug those words in a pre-made story with blank spaces.

Most often than not the final story will make you laugh!

Wanna play Mad Libs right on this site?

That's easy! Just go on to Gallery, pick one of the stories and let the fun begin!

Tip: You can also create your own Mad Lib and add it to the Gallery for others to play around with! Just click on New and let your imagination run free! Check out our special edition Topcoder & Valentine's Day Mad Lib right below.
